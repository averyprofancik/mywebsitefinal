Experiences

Here is what I've done

I am currently a Social Media Marketing intern and I couldn't love it more!

What started out my interest in social media marketing, and just marketing in general was working with Avenue A Interiors this past summer. My mother and her friend started a home staging and interior design business. They had me help with a few of their jobs this summer and I thought it was so interesting. I was not only able to help stage, but also to help create content for their social media. They are just starting out so they are in a lot of need in the marketing area. I shot pictures and videos for content for their Instagram. I also helped make Instagram stories and reels for their business. It was very simple work, however it was an awesome experience to get my feet wet in the marketing world.

I have recently had the most incredible opportunity to intern as a social media marketer for a local business called Sunny. I have been able to work with the Founders and the Social Media Manager to help get their business more up and running. I have learned such valuable skills that I would not have learned without this opportunity. I have learned how to create content, engage with followers, reach out to influencers, and so much more involving marketing. It has grown my confidence in myself and in my knowledge of the marketing world. I am excited about where we have come with Sunny and where we will go.
